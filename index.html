✨ Why Choose QuickArabic?

⚡

Instant Conversion

Press Ctrl+Space to instantly convert Latin text to beautiful Arabic script. No delays, no internet required.

🧠

Smart Mapping

Advanced algorithm that understands context. Won't convert common English letters unnecessarily while preserving Arabic accuracy.

📱

Facebook Ready

Works perfectly with Facebook posts, comments, and messages. Also compatible with Gmail, Twitter, and all websites.

🔧

Fully Customizable

Add your own transliteration rules, choose keyboard shortcuts, and customize settings for your perfect Arabic typing experience.

🌍

Universal Support

Works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Compatible with all text fields, rich editors, and content-editable areas.

🔒

Privacy First

Completely offline functionality. No data collection, no tracking, no internet required. Your text stays private.

🚀 How It Works

1

Install Extension

Download and install QuickArabic in your favorite browser in under 30 seconds.

2

Type Naturally

Go to any website and type Arabic words using English letters: "marhaba" or "shukran"

3

Press Ctrl+Space

Hit the magic shortcut and watch your text transform into beautiful Arabic script instantly.

4

Share & Enjoy

Post Arabic content on social media, send messages, or write emails effortlessly!
